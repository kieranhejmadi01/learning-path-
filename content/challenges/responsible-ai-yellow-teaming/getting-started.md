## Prepare your approach

These skills and resources are helpful before you begin:

- Proficiency in Python and PyTorch if you deploy a private model
- Familiarity with Hugging Face and LLM prompting techniques
- Access to an Arm-based cloud instance if you host a private model
- Access to a public LLM if you prefer a hosted workflow

## Resources

- Course: [Mitigating harmful consequences by the Center for Humane Technology](https://www.humanetech.com/course)

## Support and recognition

This challenge is designed to be self-serve, with optional community support from Arm Ambassadors in the Arm Developer program. If you are not already part of the program, you can [join here](https://www.arm.com/resources/developer-program?#register).

Strong community contributions can earn digital badges that support CV or resumé building and help you show your work publicly.
